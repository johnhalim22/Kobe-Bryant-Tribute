Tribute to Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Here is a list of his accomplishments:

  • Drafted by the Charlotte Hornets and traded to the Los Angeles Lakers in 1996.
  • Scored 81 points against the Toronto Raptors in January, 2006.
  • Became the 3rd leading scorer in NBA history.
  • All-time leading scorer in Lakers history.
  • became the only player to play 20 years for a single NBA franchise.
  • Retired in 2016
